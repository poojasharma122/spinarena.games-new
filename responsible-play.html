Responsible Play

At Spin Arena, we believe gaming should always be fun, safe, and entertaining. While our platform is designed for social play and does not involve real money, we still encourage responsible habits to ensure that your time with us remains positive.

Our Commitment

  • Provide tools that help players stay in control of their gaming activity.
  • Promote safe gaming practices and raise awareness of potential risks.
  • Offer clear access to resources for those who may need support.

Tips for Responsible Play

  • Set a time limit before you start playing and stick to it.
  • Treat Spin Arena games as entertainment, not as a way to make money.
  • Take regular breaks to keep gameplay balanced with other activities.
  • Never use Spin Arena as a substitute for work, family, or personal responsibilities.
  • If you feel gaming is becoming a problem, seek help immediately.

Tools We Provide

  • Time Reminders – Optional alerts to track how long you’ve been playing.
  • Token Limits – Options to set daily limits on play tokens.
  • Self-Exclusion – Ability to temporarily or permanently close your account.
  • Newsletter Control – Opt in or out of promotional emails at any time.

Age Restrictions

Spin Arena is strictly for players aged 18+ (or the legal age in your jurisdiction). We do not allow underage users to register or participate.

Where to Find Help

If you are concerned about your gaming habits or someone else’s, please consider reaching out to the following organizations:

  • Gamblers Anonymous – www.gamblersanonymous.org
  • GamCare – www.gamcare.org.uk
  • National Council on Problem Gambling – www.ncpgambling.org

Our Message
